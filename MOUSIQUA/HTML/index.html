Yudi Watanabe
Brazilian-Japanese

Yudi Watanabe is a renownedBrazilian DJ and music producer of Japanese descent, known for his ability to create and mix different musical genres. Born in Campinas - SP, Brazil. He has performed in the main clubs in São Paulo such as D-Edge, Caos,Club 88 gaining recognition for his energetic live performances that captivate audiences and take them on an exciting musical journey. His sets are marked by careful track selection, smooth transitions and an infectious atmosphere that keeps people dancing the night away.

In addition to gigs, Yudi began to stand out as a music producer, releasing original tracks and remixes on influential labels in the Progressive House scene such as Mango Alley, Deepwibe Underground, 3rd Avenue, Transensations, Clubsonica, Freegrant, One Of a Kind, SoundTeller and many others. others.

Their musical style spans a wide range of genres, including house, techno and trance, combining catchy elements and pulsating beats to create a progressive sound experience, winning over fans in different countries. His music has been praised and supported by the most influential DJs in the electronic scene such as Hernan Cattaneo, DJ Ogawa, Dj Ruby, Analog Jungs, Subandrio, Navar, Kamilo Sanclemente, Andre Moret, Luciano Scheffer, Ca Cimino, Da Luka, Topo Larocca, Gui Milani and many others.

Yudi Watanabe explores new sounds and musical techniques to offer his fans unique and exciting music experiences. His dedication and essence to his art is evident in every project he is involved in, and his name is now synonymous with excellence in the electronic music scene. With a career on the rise, his continues to delight and inspire audiences with her music and stage charisma. He’s been a music producer for 10 years and is now launching his international Tour.His fans are affectionately named YUYU FANS.
